Award

Finishing Fellowship Award, Summer 2026

May 2026 · Michigan Technological University

I was selected as one of the recipients of the Graduate School's Finishing Fellowship Award for Summer 2026. The fellowship, granted by the Graduate Dean Awards Advisory Panel, supports doctoral students in the final stage of their programs so they can devote their full attention to completing the dissertation.

I am grateful to the panel, to the Physics Department, and to my advisor, Prof. Issei Nakamura, for their support and nomination. The fellowship allows me to focus fully on finishing my dissertation on the molecular origins of viscosity in ionic and polymerized ionic liquids ahead of my defense on July 27, 2026.

The Graduate School's announcement is posted on the Graduate School blog.
