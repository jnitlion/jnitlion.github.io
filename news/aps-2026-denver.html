Two Talks at the APS Global Physics Summit in Denver

March 2026 · Denver, CO

I presented at the American Physical Society Global Physics Summit in Denver, CO, contributing to two talks from our group's work on polymerized ionic liquids:

  • “Role of Cation Size and Dipolar Motion Restriction in the Viscosity of Polymerized Ionic Liquids” — my talk on the polyIL viscosity project.
  • “Polymerized Stockmayer Fluid MD Simulation of Ion Transport in Polymeric Ionic Liquid–LiTFSI Blends” — presented with Prof. Issei Nakamura and our Sandia collaborators.

This was my fourth consecutive year presenting at the APS March Meeting / Global Physics Summit, going back to Las Vegas 2023.
