Award

Finalist at the Midwest Regional 3-Minute Thesis Competition

March 2026 · Kansas City, MO

I represented Michigan Tech at the regional (Midwest) 3-Minute Thesis Competition, organized by the Midwestern Association of Graduate Schools in Kansas City, MO, and advanced to the final round alongside 9 other finalists from across the region.

The 3MT challenge: explain years of doctoral research — in my case, the molecular origins of viscosity in ionic liquids — to a general audience in three minutes, with a single static slide. Earning the spot followed a 1st-place win at Michigan Tech's own 3MT the previous November.
