Publication

Paper Published in The Journal of Chemical Physics

July 2025 · with A.L. Frischknecht, M.J. Stevens, and I. Nakamura

Our paper, “Stockmayer Fluid Simulations for Viscosity and Glass Transition Temperature of Ionic Liquids,” appeared in The Journal of Chemical Physics 163, 044503 (2025) — doi:10.1063/5.0268727.

The work shows that a minimal coarse-grained model — spheres with a charge and a dipole — reproduces the experimental viscosity and glass-transition behavior of ethylammonium nitrate, validating a fast, transferable platform for exploring ionic-liquid design space. It forms the foundation of my dissertation; a full summary is on the research page.
