Milestone

Dissertation Defense Scheduled: July 27, 2026

July 2026 · Michigan Technological University

I will defend my PhD dissertation, “Molecular Origins of Viscosity in Ionic and Polymerized Ionic Liquids: Insights from Stockmayer Fluid Simulations,” on July 27, 2026 at Michigan Technological University.

The dissertation, advised by Prof. Issei Nakamura, spans five years of work on coarse-grained molecular dynamics of charged soft matter: from validating the Stockmayer model against ethylammonium nitrate, through a systematic map of ionic-liquid parameter space, to viscosity scaling and ion pairing in polymerized ionic liquids, in collaboration with researchers at Sandia National Laboratories.
